Common questions

Does ParkiWell work offline?

Yes. Your records live on your device and stay available without a connection. If you use an account, changes sync automatically when you're back online.

How do I delete my account?

In the app: Profile → Settings → Delete account. This removes your account and synced records.

Is my health information shared?

No. We don't sell data, show ads, or share your records with advertisers. See the Privacy Policy for details.
