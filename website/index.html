A calmer way to manage Parkinson’s care

ParkiWell brings symptoms, medication routines, and guided speech and movement practice together in one supportive place — and it works even when you're offline.

Coming soon to the App Store and Google Play.

What ParkiWell does

  • See your day clearly — log symptoms in seconds and keep medication schedules in one timeline.
  • Practice with a plan — set an approachable weekly goal for speech and movement sessions with guided videos.
  • Notice patterns over time — simple on-device trends across weeks and months.
  • Gentle by design — calm colors, large touch targets, reduced-motion support, and full dark mode.

ParkiWell is an organizational tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
